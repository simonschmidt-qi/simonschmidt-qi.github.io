I am a postdoctoral researcher at Ruhr University Bochum, at the Chair of Quantum Computing of [Jonas Haferkamp](https://sites.google.com/view/jonas-haferkamp/home). Before, I was part of the research group of [Michael Walter](https://michaelwalter.info/walter).

My research interests are broadly in quantum information theory, quantum cryptography and quantum groups. I work on nonlocal games and **self-testing** to explore how to certify proper functioning of an uncharacterized quantum device. Closely related are **compiled nonlocal games**, in which the nonlocality assumption is replaced by a computational assumption, using cryptography. In addition, I work on the isomorphism game and **quantum automorphism groups** of finite graphs. See also [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.de/citations?hl=de&user=jclf4TAAAAAJ) or below for an overview of my publications.
